WANTED

Carlos San Francisco


Chief Bossman

"Greetings Gumshoe. It's up to you to catch the most notorious thief of cultural

artifacts in the world! He's been taunting us since his last heist, sending facts

and images about where he's hiding. This criminal has been a thorn in my side for

years. We've never been able to catch him. I'm counting on you to bring him in!

Game Instructions

You are tasked with bringing in Carlos San Francisco. You will be deployed where he last struck. From there you will need to use the clues hes been leaving to deduce where in the world he might be hiding. If you are on the right track he'll send a new clue. If you are losing track of him you'll have to use the clues you already have. Be careful, he only gave us a set amount of time to track him down before he pulls off another heist. The time you spend investigating and traveling will count against you. Once you are sure of where he is you must use our patented Circular Analog Radial Modual for Earth Navigation or C.A.R.M.E.N. to get an up close view of the city where he's hiding. If you get close enough you should be able to track him down and our team will move in. Good luck out there!
