WANTED

Carlos San Francisco


Chief Bossman

"Greetings Gumshoe. It's up to you to catch the most notorious thief of cultural

artifacts in the world! He's been taunting us since his last heist, sending facts

and images about where he's hiding. This criminal has been a thorn in my side for

years. We've never been able to catch him. I'm counting on you to bring him in!

Game Instructions

You will start where Carlos struck last. You will have 72 hours to try and track him down. Your travel time will be deducted from this clock and if your clock reaches zero he has gotten away. You must travel from location to location to try and find out where he is hiding. If you get a new clue you have gotten closer to him from where you were. If not, you have gotten further away from him. Once you determine where he is you must travel to that location. In order to catch him you will need to ZOOM IN to activate C.A.R.M.E.N. (the Circumference Angled Region Module for Earth Navigation) which will allow you to find Carlos if he is in the same location as you. Good luck.
